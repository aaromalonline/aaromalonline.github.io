Deep Generative Models can learn the underlying spatial boundaries of massive, unstructured datasets.

Generative Adversarial Networks

A game-theoretic setup where a Generator $G$ tries to fool a Discriminator $D$. The Minimax objective:

def gan_loss(D_real, D_fake):
    real_loss = -torch.mean(torch.log(D_real))
    fake_loss = -torch.mean(torch.log(1 - D_fake))
    return real_loss + fake_loss
